Revenue Strategy

  • Diagnostic — cash-flow entry point. Low resistance, immediate revenue. Priced $149–$499.
  • Battery Retrofit — primary profit driver. One close = $3,299 to company.
  • Monitoring + Panel Cleaning — recurring revenue. 100 active accounts = $1,579/mo passive income. Cleaning 2×/year included; cost absorbed into company margin to keep price competitive at $49.99/mo.
  • Partner / Add-On Leads — leads outside current scope (roofing, HVAC, EV charging, new installs for unqualified customers) can be sold or closed through vetted partners, increasing revenue per customer without adding operational overhead.
  • Key lever: Improving diagnostic-to-retrofit conversion from 20% → 35% roughly doubles revenue without a single new lead.
2 — Target Customer

Primary

Southern California homeowners with an existing solar system (installed 2010–2020) who:

  • Are still paying high utility bills despite having solar
  • Have never had their system professionally inspected
  • Don't know if their system is working at full capacity
  • Are interested in battery backup due to wildfires / outages

Secondary

Homeowners considering solar for the first time. Dennis has 10+ years operating in new solar installation — this remains an active service line alongside diagnostics and retrofits.

5 — Go-to-Market Strategy
Phase 1 — Now → Month 3
Goal: 10–20 diagnostics/month · 2–4 battery retrofits/month
  • Field outreach — Ronda + techs knock doors in high solar-density neighborhoods. Target homes with visible panels in Chula Vista, El Cajon, Santee, Poway, Escondido.
  • Referral seeding — every completed diagnostic = ask for 2 referrals.
  • GHL follow-up automation — every booked eval gets automated reminders. Every no-close gets a 30/60/90-day nurture sequence.
  • Google Business Profile — fully optimized with photos, services, and active review responses.
Phase 2 — Month 2 → Month 6
Goal: 30–50 diagnostics/month · 8–12 retrofits/month
  • Google Local Services Ads (LSA) — "Solar inspection San Diego," "solar battery backup," "solar not working." Pay per lead, not per click. Highest ROI for a local service business.
  • SEO landing pages — /solar-diagnostic-san-diego, /battery-backup-installation, /powerwall-installer-san-diego targeting high-intent keyword clusters.
  • Facebook / Instagram — before/after content, educational reels (why solar systems degrade), testimonials. Retarget site visitors with offer ads.
Phase 3 — Month 6+
Goal: $50K+/month revenue · multiple reps · expand to LA/OC
  • Hire 2–3 additional field setters.
  • Launch monitoring subscription service.
  • Explore solar financing partnerships to increase battery retrofit close rate.
  • Expand to LA and Orange County with local ops partners.
