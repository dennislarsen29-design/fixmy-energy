Commission Formula

Fixed Revenue − Fixed Costs = Gross Commission  ×  40% = Rep Pay

Rep earns 40%, company keeps 60% of gross commission on every transaction. No base salary, draw, or guaranteed minimum.

Revenue Strategy

  • Diagnostic — cash-flow entry point. Low resistance, immediate revenue. Priced $149–$499.
  • Battery Retrofit — primary profit driver. One close = $3,299 to company.
  • Monitoring — recurring revenue. 100 active accounts = $2,580/mo passive income.
  • Key lever: Improving diagnostic-to-retrofit conversion from 20% → 35% roughly doubles revenue without a single new lead.
